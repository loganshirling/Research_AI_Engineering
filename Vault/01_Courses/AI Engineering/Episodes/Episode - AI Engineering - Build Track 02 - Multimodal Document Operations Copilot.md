# Episode - AI Engineering - Build Track 02 - Multimodal Document Operations Copilot

## Goal

Track the teaching artifact for the second build-track capstone: a multimodal document operations copilot.

## Source note

- [[Practice - End-to-End Build - Multimodal Document Operations Copilot]]

## Intended learning outcome

After this episode, the learner should be able to explain:
- why document AI needs page understanding in addition to corpus retrieval,
- how structured extraction differs from question answering,
- why confidence handling and review routing matter,
- and how page-level evidence should stay attached to extracted claims.

## Notes

This episode should emphasize that multimodal document systems are not just “RAG with PDFs.”
It should make the distinction between:
- retrieval,
- page interpretation,
- structured extraction,
- and human review.
