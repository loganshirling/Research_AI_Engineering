## Goal

Track the NotebookLM-style teaching artifact for the practitioner systems foundations portion of the AI Engineering course.

## Intended learning outcome

After this episode, the learner should be able to:
- explain tokens, embeddings, transformers, prefill, and decode at a practical level,
- describe how retrieval design affects RAG quality,
- explain why hybrid search and reranking are often good defaults,
- compare models using a scorecard instead of vibes,
- and explain why latency, validation, and observability are core AI engineering concerns.

## Notes

This episode should feel foundations-heavy and practitioner-oriented.

It should compress architecture, retrieval, model selection, latency, safety, and observability into one coherent introduction rather than a scattered survey.
