# Episode - AI Engineering - Build Track 01 - Internal Research Assistant

## Goal

Track the teaching artifact for the first build-track capstone: an evidence-grounded internal research assistant.

## Source note

- [[Practice - End-to-End Build - Internal Research Assistant]]

## Intended learning outcome

After this episode, the learner should be able to explain:
- why grounded research systems need explicit retrieval and citation discipline,
- what should stay deterministic in a research assistant,
- how evals and rollback logic protect answer quality,
- and why false confidence is the dominant failure cost for this build.

## Notes

This episode should emphasize the difference between:
- search,
- evidence synthesis,
- answer construction,
- and optional writing handoff.

The build should sound reviewable and operational, not magical.
