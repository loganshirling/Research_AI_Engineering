# Episode - AI Engineering - Section 02 - Advanced Systems and Agent Operations

## Goal

Track the NotebookLM-style teaching artifact for the advanced systems and agent operations section of the AI Engineering course.

## Source packet

## Intended learning outcome

After this episode, the learner should be able to explain:
- why advanced AI systems are mostly about control over state, tools, and failure modes,
- what should stay deterministic in an agent workflow,
- why approvals and permissions are product requirements rather than afterthoughts,
- how multimodal document systems differ from text-only retrieval systems,
- and why voice systems introduce a different latency and recovery problem than standard chat systems.
