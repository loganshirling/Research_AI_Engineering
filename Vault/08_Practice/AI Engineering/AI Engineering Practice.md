# AI Engineering Practice

## Purpose

This note is the entry point for the AI Engineering practice layer.

The topic notes explain concepts. The practice notes force you to use those concepts to make decisions under uncertainty:
- choose the right lever,
- define the right eval,
- diagnose the real bottleneck,
- and avoid solving the wrong problem elegantly.

## How to use these notes

For each drill:
1. answer from first principles before looking at the rubric,
2. write down the failure mode you think is most likely,
3. identify the next measurement or experiment you would run,
4. compare your answer against the success criteria,
5. note what assumption was wrong.

## What good progress looks like

You are improving when you can:
- tell a knowledge problem from a behavior problem,
- distinguish model issues from system-design issues,
- define an eval before proposing a change,
- explain what data would change your mind,
- and propose a rollback-safe next step instead of a vague optimization idea.
